About Us

Computer Engineering provides the opportunity to work in the continuously changing technology sector. With the development of faster hardware components, new communication systems and software there is a demand for computer Engineers. Computer Engineering students study the design of digital hardware and software systems. Computer Engineering encompasses a wide range of topics including operating systems, computer architecture, computer networks, robotics, artificial intelligence and computer-aided design. An important area within computer engineering is the development of embedded system devices such as cell phones, digital audio players, digital video recorders, alarm systems, x-ray machines and laser surgical tools which require integration of hardware and embedded software and these are all the result of computer engineering. Software engineering companies, telecommunications firms, designers of digital hardware and many other business enterprises hire Computer Engineers right out of college and pay them well..

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs): A graduate in Computer Engineering is expected to:-

  • Gather requirements, design and implement software projects using modern tools.
  • Solve real time problems by providing automated solutions in diverse fields of human endeavour.
  • Optimize the use of resources in problem solving and work as a team player.

As we look ahead to the future, PHCET remains committed to fostering an inclusive, innovative, and forward-thinking environment that prepares our students to tackle the challenges of the modern world. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey of discovery, growth, and achievement. Together, we will shape a brighter tomorrow. Welcome to PHCET.

Services

Studies at PHCET go beyond traditional classroom learning. We believe in a holistic approach to education that encompasses a wide range of experiences, including seminars and interactive sessions. Through these enriching experiences, we empower our students to become well-rounded individuals who are not only well-versed in their chosen disciplines but also equipped with the skills and confidence to thrive in the real world.

Curriculum Development

The department develops and updates the curriculum for its academic programs to stay current with advancements in the field. This includes designing courses, selecting textbooks, and integrating new technologies.

Teaching and Instructions

Faculty members in the department teach courses, lead labs, conduct tutorials, and assess students' progress through assignments, exams, and projects.

Research and Innovations

Faculty members and students collaborate on projects related to computer architecture, embedded systems, digital signal processing, robotics, artificial intelligence, and more.

Community Engagement

Computer Engineering department engages with the local community through outreach programs, coding camps, hackathons, and other initiatives to promote computer engineering and STEM education.

Seminars and Workshops

The department organizes seminars, workshops, and guest lectures by experts from academia and industry. These events provide students and faculty with exposure to the latest trends and developments in the field.

Student Support

Computer Department provide academic advising, career counseling, and support services to students. They also offer resources for job placement and internship opportunities.
